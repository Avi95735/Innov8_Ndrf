Some Wonderful Achievements Of NDRF :


NDRF saved 6,000 lives in 900 operations conducted in 2023: Director General Atul Karwal.

Atul Karwal also said the NDRF personnel also rescued 51,000 people as well as 3,000 animals in various operations conducted last year.


Assistance during the COVID-19 Pandemic (2020-2021) :


NDRF teams assisted in managing the pandemic by setting up quarantine facilities, distributing essential supplies like food, drinking water bottles, medicines, and supporting health care systems.




Rescue and Relief during Cyclone Amphan (2020):


NDRF teams were deployed to West Bengal and other affected regions to assist in rescue operations, provide immediate relief, and support recovery efforts following Cyclone Amphan.




Assistance in the Kerala Floods (2018):


During the massive floods in Kerala, NDRF teams were actively involved in rescue operations, saving lives, and providing relief to the affected population.




Earthquake Relief in Nepal (2015):


NDRF teams provided vital support during the aftermath of the devastating earthquake in Nepal, including rescue operations, medical aid, and assistance in rebuilding efforts.




Rescue Operations during the Uttarakhand Floods (2013):


The NDRF played a crucial role in rescuing thousands of people stranded by the devastating floods in Uttarakhand. Their efforts included airlifting survivors and providing aid in severely affected areas.




Rescue Efforts in Mumbai during the 2005 Floods:


The NDRF was instrumental in the rescue and relief operations during the severe flooding in Mumbai, which caused widespread damage and disruption.
